About Me

Hello! I am Priyanka Sharma, a final semester graduate student at the Israel Institute of Technology (Technion). My research focuses on the cooling of hypersonic leading edges using advanced measurement techniques in thermo-fluids dynamics and multi-phase flows.

Research Interests

  • Hypersonic Aerodynamics
  • Thermo-fluids Dynamics
  • Multi-phase Flows
  • Fluid Mechanics of Liquid Sheet Jets

My current research involves developing experimental facilities and using shadowgraph imaging to characterize water sheets under different conditions. I aim to size the liquid sheets, investigate their dynamics, and identify transition conditions between jet and spray regimes.
